About Us

Who we are

We’re a professional and highly rated organization that leading in the field of fitness and gymnasium, delivering a wide range of well-prepared service and high-quality products including body-shaped programme, nutrition product, gym accessories and equipment.

Founded in 2022, MY GYM is operating well throughout Malaysia with a diversified and dedicated team of staff, athletes, and proactive influencers.

Every day we aim to inspire individuals of all ages and genders to believe in their fitness potential, then fuel them to achieve it.

What we do

At MY GYM, our mission is to help the people accomplish their goals — making the best in our fitness centre available to everyone, whatever their goal. We pride ourselves in providing a broad selection of programmes, nutrition supply and equipment at exceptional price to power this, whether he or she is a novice or elite in this career.

We believe fitness and gym should be accessible to everyone. That’s why we keep invent new programmes that are more effective and attractive compared to previous, with suggestions and advices from our trainees.

Our coaches and trainers that grouped by elites and professionals also do the best to make sure fitness and gym newbies can get their plan started as soon as possible with assistance of nutrition products and workout practice at home.

What we have

Our facility is filled with the best strength and cardio equipment — all the essentials you need for a great workout experience. We have world-class personal trainers with you to adventure all the awesomeness that My Gym had offer with an incredibly friendly price.

To encourage workout from home in this pandemic era, we also serve trade of reliable equipment such as bench, dumbbell, barbell etc. Besides, we serve supplement such as protein, creatine, glutamine, energetic supplement, more and more.
